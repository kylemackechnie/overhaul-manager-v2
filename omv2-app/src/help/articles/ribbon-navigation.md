# Ribbon Navigation

The ribbon is the strip across the top of the app, modelled on Microsoft Office.

## How it works

The ribbon has two rows:

- **Tab row** — module groups (Project, Cost Tracking, Personnel, HSE, Subcontractors, Logistics, Hardware, Tooling, Site Specific, Global)
- **Button strip** — context-sensitive buttons for the active tab

Clicking a tab swaps the strip. Clicking a button navigates to that panel.

## The File menu

The purple **File** button on the far left opens a dropdown with global features:

- User Management
- Audit Trail
- Reports Database
- Save Backup
- Help & Guide
- Sign Out

These aren't project-scoped — they apply across the whole app.

## Customising the ribbon

You can reorder and hide tabs to match how you work:

- Drag tabs to reorder them
- Click ⚙ on the far right to hide tabs you don't use
- Hidden tabs go into a ··· overflow menu

Your customisations are saved per-user and follow you across devices.
